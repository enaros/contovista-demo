Why Contovista?

Seamless Integration

We seamlessly integrate into your existing e-Banking and Mobile banking and fully customise to your needs.

Shared Learning

We achieve an outstandingly high categorisation rate by use of our hybrid architecture and a learning effect amongst all our banks. What’s more: our banks benefit from an always up-to-date database.

Time to Market

Easy and fast integration (2-5 months) due to our standard software with multiple data-adapters (Finnova, Avaloq, Viseca…) and frontend integrations.

Made in Switzerland

We design and develop our solutions in Switzerland – the country of banks.

Dedicated Team

Our passionate team supports you throughout the entire journey in order to ensure a delightful experience.

One Step Ahead

We invest a lot of time thinking about a Data Driven Future in Banking. Talk to us about your ideas! We might already have a solution… or at least an opinion.
